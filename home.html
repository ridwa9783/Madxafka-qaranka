About

Preserving Somali History and Culture

Matxafka Qaranka Soomaaliyeed is dedicated to collecting, preserving, and exhibiting Somali artifacts, historical documents, and cultural treasures. It serves as a hub for education and cultural appreciation.

Visitors can explore permanent and temporary exhibits that highlight Somali traditions, art, and heritage. Our museum aims to educate, inspire, and connect people with Somalia's rich cultural legacy.

Learn more about our mission and upcoming exhibits on our official website. Join us in celebrating and preserving Somali heritage for future generations.
